# Research team — distilled knowledge

> The Research team's **settled** findings: the conclusions, with their confidence and primary
> sources — without the raw log. research-head curates this. An agent reads this to build on what
> research already established instead of re-running it. Every claim carries a real, checkable source;
> nothing is upgraded from "[verify]" to fact without being verified.

## Settled findings
_(Each: the conclusion, confidence (low/medium/high), and the primary source(s). Filled in as research lands.)_

## Domain / market picture
_(What the real need is, what already exists, where the gaps are — the scope-shaping context. Anything scope-expanding here has been gated by critics against the Charter before it counts as settled.)_

## Open questions / genuinely mixed evidence
_(What research could not resolve, and where the evidence is honestly split. A named open question is a feature.)_
